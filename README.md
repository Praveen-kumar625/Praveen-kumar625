## 🏗️ Featured Engineering Projects

### 🚨 RoadSOS (ASTRAX) | *National Road Safety Hackathon Project*
*An AI-powered emergency response platform designed for citizen safety.*
*   **Problem:** Delayed emergency response due to manual accident reporting.
*   **Solution:** Engineered an automated crash detection system utilizing smartphone IMU data to trigger instant alerts.
*   **Tech Stack:** React.js, Node.js, Python, MongoDB.
*   **Impact:** Reduced conceptual response time by streamlining emergency service dispatch logic.

### 🏥 Rapid Crisis Response | *Hospitality & Emergency Sector*
*A tactical emergency coordination system.*
*   **Problem:** Reliance on stable internet during localized crises in the hospitality sector.
*   **Solution:** Built an offline-first resilient platform featuring 3D situational awareness mapping.
*   **Tech Stack:** React, Node.js, Gemini AI Integration.
*   **Impact:** Enabled continuous tactical coordination without active internet connectivity.

### 🌍 Sustainability Intelligence Hub
*A global risk platform for monitoring supply chains.*
*   **Problem:** Unpredictable climate disruptions affecting logistics.
*   **Solution:** Developed an analytics pipeline to predict disruptions using advanced data modeling.
*   **Tech Stack:** Databricks Lakehouse, Gemini AI, Python.
*   **Impact:** Provided actionable insights for supply chain resilience through predictive AI modeling.
